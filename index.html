QRCodeSnap: Your Menu Bar QR Code Scanner

Say goodbye to tedious screenshots and switching apps. QRCodeSnap is a lightweight, efficient macOS app that lives in your menu bar, allowing you to capture any QR code on your screen with a single click.

Instant Capture, Unbelievably Simple

Are you tired of the hassle of scanning QR codes from your screen? Whether it's from a webpage, a document, or an application, just click the QRCodeSnap icon in your menu bar. It will instantly scan the entire screen, find, and recognize all QR codes for you. It's hassle-free and all about efficiency.

Everything You Need, All in One Place

QRCodeSnap offers a range of customizable settings to perfect your user experience. Set it to launch at login so it's always ready, enable sound notifications for instant feedback on every successful scan, or choose to receive results directly via a system notification. We've thought of every detail.
